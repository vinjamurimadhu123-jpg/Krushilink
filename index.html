Digital platforms fail due to digital illiteracy. ## Solution KrushiLink provides: - Consumer mobile app - Low-cost GSM-based button device for farmers - AI-driven demand forecasting and pricing engine ## Tech Stack - Flutter (Mobile App) - Node.js / Firebase (Backend) - Python (AI Models) - GSM IoT Device ## Impact - Fair pricing - Reduced food wastage - Digital inclusion - Sustainable AI-driven agriculture
